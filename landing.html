FAQ

Questions answered.

Will my WhatsApp account get banned?+
No. WABlast uses the official Meta WhatsApp Business API — the same technology used by banks and large retailers. Your account is fully protected and compliant.
Do messages come from my own number?+
Yes. Messages are sent via your registered WhatsApp Business number. Your clients see your name and number and can reply directly to you — no middleman.
Do my contacts need to save my number first?+
With the official Meta Business API, no. Messages are delivered to any valid WhatsApp number without the recipient needing to save your number first.
What format does my spreadsheet need to be?+
Any Excel (.xlsx), CSV, or TXT file works. WABlast automatically detects your Name and Cell columns. SA numbers starting with 0 are converted to +27 automatically.
What happens when I use all my messages?+
Extra messages are billed at a per-message rate depending on your plan (R0.25–R0.55 each). You'll receive a notification before you hit your limit so there are no surprises.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes, cancel anytime from your dashboard with no penalties or cancellation fees. Your subscription remains active until the end of your billing period.
How do I pay?+
We use PayFast — South Africa's leading payment gateway. Pay by credit card, instant EFT, or regular EFT. All prices are in South African Rand and include VAT.
